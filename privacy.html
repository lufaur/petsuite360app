Privacy Policy

PetSuite 360 - Complete Pet Care Companion

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Introduction

PetSuite 360 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Pet Information: Names, breeds, birthdates, photos, and health records you enter for your pets
  • Care Logs: Feeding schedules, medications, vet visits, and activity records
  • Reminders: Notifications you set for pet care activities
  • App Preferences: Your settings and customization choices

All this data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and is not transmitted to our servers.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your pet data will be stored in your private iCloud account. This data:

  • Is encrypted by Apple
  • Is accessible only to you through your Apple ID
  • Syncs across your personal devices
  • Is not accessible by PetSuite 360 developers

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not have access to your pet data

Apple Services

PetSuite 360 uses the following Apple services:

  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud sync (your private Apple iCloud)
  • Notifications: Local notifications for reminders (no data sent externally)

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • Apple's secure enclave technology
  • iCloud encryption (if sync is enabled)
  • App sandboxing that prevents other apps from accessing your data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Export: You can export your pet data at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual records or all data from Settings
  • Portability: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

Children's Privacy

PetSuite 360 does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and pet owners of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
