Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new pet?

Tap the "+" button on the Dashboard or go to Pets tab and tap "Add Pet". Fill in your pet's details including name, species, breed, and birthday. You can also add a photo from your library or camera.

How do I set up reminders?

Go to the Reminders tab and tap "+" to create a new reminder. Choose the pet, activity type (feeding, medication, vet visit, etc.), and set your preferred time and frequency. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings.

How does iCloud sync work?

When iCloud is enabled, your pet data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID. Go to Settings > iCloud Sync to enable this feature. Your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account.

How do I log a care activity?

From the Dashboard, tap "Quick Log" or go to your pet's profile and tap "Add Log". Select the activity type (meal, walk, medication, grooming, etc.), add any notes, and save. Activities are timestamped automatically.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks: unlimited pets (free is limited to 2), advanced health tracking, custom reminder schedules, detailed reports and insights, photo galleries, and family sharing. You can try Premium free for 7 days.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple: Open Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > PetSuite 360 > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Data & Privacy > Export Data. Choose your preferred format and the data you want to include. The export file will be saved to your Files app or can be shared directly.

Can I share pet care with family?

Yes! With Premium, enable Family Sharing through iCloud. All family members on your Apple Family plan can access and update pet information, ensuring everyone stays coordinated on pet care.

How do I delete my data?

To delete individual items, swipe left on any pet, log, or reminder and tap Delete. To delete all data, go to Settings > Data & Privacy > Delete All Data. This action cannot be undone.

The app isn't sending notifications

Check these settings: 1) Device Settings > PetSuite 360 > Notifications must be ON. 2) Device Settings > Focus modes shouldn't block the app. 3) In-app Settings > Notifications should be enabled. If issues persist, try reinstalling the app.
